Encore

Take a moment. Close your eyes and imagine this: A ginormous crowd, thronging around the stage, the place jam-packed, voices of thousands, almost fanatical in nature, with just one name on their lips. Thousands of passionate fans going wild on hearing the first notes of music, on their way to reaching heights of exhilaration which only music can offer. There, we defined ENCORE for you! Over the last couple of years, Incident has hosted nostalgic performances by the likes of Sunidhi Chauhan, KK, Javed Ali, Kunal Ganjawala, Indian Ocean, Raghu Dixit Project and many more. This year we are back with our promise to deliver mind blowing performances. The dynamic duo who have gifted us with numbers for movies such as Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi, Chak De! India, Fashion, Heroine, Ladies vs. Ricky Bahl, Band Baaja Baaraat, we present to you SALIM - SULAIMAN!! This March 9, be assured of a never ending hypnotism!
