About Us
Find your inner self in the serene sands of NITK...

National Institute of Technology Surathkal, Karnataka, was founded in 1960 on the pristine sands of the west coast of India. With its own private beach, which includes a lighthouse, NITK lies just an hour north of Mangalore, on the NH 66. Its expansive campus houses over five thousand students from all across the country, making this an extremely diverse place. NITK has consistently been voted among the best engineering colleges in India. Apart from the usual academic excellence, the students of NITK organize themselves into many technical and non-technical clubs and conduct various events throughout the year. But, the biggest annual events here would be Engineer, our technical symposium, and Incident, our cultural festival, both of which are completely overseen by the students of the college from the feet up.

Incident, the annual cultural fest of NITK, was started way back in the 80's and has grown bigger ever since. This year, we are reminiscing about those good old retro days, and we invite everyone to come and enjoy the celebration with us. Incident has become a permanent event on the calendar of every college student in the state. It has been bringing together great artists and performers, such as Led Zeppelica, MotherJane, Breathe The Floyd, Sunidhi Chauhan and countless others; and it has seen some truly memorable and magnificent performances. With an inexhaustible array of exciting events, a beach where you can kick back and relax and an invigorating and thrilling basketball competition, Slam Dunk, we aim to keep you engaged and entertained. And with the huge number of prizes which are there to be won, nothing should be holding you back from getting on a train and racing over to our sands.
