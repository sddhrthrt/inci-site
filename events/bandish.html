Bandish

Sensational voices combine with inspiring melodies
to create resonating music.
Bandish shall prove to be an evening
of majestic performances
which will enthral the crowds.

Pre-Registration is compulsory

Rules

1. No. of participants: Min 4, Maximum 18.
2. One person cannot be a part of more than one band in the same event.
3. Time Limit: (15+5)* minutes time on stage inclusive of set up. Penalty will be imposed on exceeding the time limit.
4. A minimum of 3 participants should be on stage at any point of time, involved in the song as vocalists or instrumentalists.
5. There is no restriction on the number of songs and the teams are free to perform as many songs as they can manage to fit into the time-slot.
6. Songs can be in any Indian language.
7. Semi classical compositions are permitted.
8. No pre-recorded sequence can be played on stage.
9. All teams are expected to be present before the start of the event,
when the order of performances will be scheduled on a lots basis.
If the teams (or a representative from them) fail to show up,
the organizers will not take responsibility for teams missing their designated slots.
10. Judging of the event will be based on the tightness of the band,
the selection of material, the individual skills of instrumentalists,
and the overall effect
including audience appeal and appreciation.
11. A stage will be provided, with electrical connections
sufficient to service an 8-piece band.
Monitors will be provided on the stage,
as well as up to 4 vocal microphones.
A standard drum kit and a keyboard
will be provided. Besides these, any other equipment,
as per individual requirements will have to be brought
along by the teams themselves.
The same applies to guitar distortion
boxes and similar pieces of equipment.
12. Online Registration is mandatory for this event.
 Spot registrations will not be entertained.
* Subject to change depending on the number of participating bands.
Teams are requested come prepared with additional songs
(additional 10 minutes worth) just in case the time slots are extended.

Please carry your college ID.
