Pulse

Incident 2014 proudly presents Pulse, a Western Rock competition for bands to showcase their prowess. From funky grooves, to thumping bass lines and tight drum beats, Pulse will quench your thirst for rock and heavy metal.

subtitle: Rules firstpara: | 1. No. of participants: Min 3, Maximum 8.
2. One person cannot participate in more than one band in the same event.
3. Time Limit: 12+3 minutes on stage inclusive of set up.
Penalty will be imposed on exceeding the time limit.
4. There is no restriction on the number of songs and the teams are free
to perform as many songs as they can manage to fit into the time-slot.
5. All teams are expected to be present before the start of the event,
when the order of performances will be scheduled on a lots basis. 
If the teams (or a representative from them) fail to show up,
the organizers will not take responsibility for teams
missing their designated slots.
6. Judging of the event will be based on the usual rock competition criteria -
the tightness of the band, the selection of material,
the individual skills of instrumentalists and vocals,
and the overall effect including
audience appeal and appreciation.
7. Standard drum kit, 3 amps and 4 microphones will be provided.
8. Online Registration is mandatory for this event. Spot registrations will not be allowed.
9. The participation for PULSE 2013 will be decided through a screening procedure.
Each band must send in a video or audio recording of one
original/cover before 21st February 12 p.m.to
musicals@incident.co.in.
